notes, frameworks, and work product from my nyu stern part-time mba. organized by course so i can reach for them at will.

summer 2026

  • Foundations of Finance Prof. Anthony Lynch · COR1-GB.2311 · Mon/Wed 6–9pm · May 18 – Jun 29, 2026 (Midterm Jun 15, Final Jun 29) Financial markets and asset valuation: time value of money, equity portfolio theory (mean-variance, CAPM, multifactor), fixed income (no-arbitrage, yield curves), derivatives (options, forwards, Black-Scholes), market efficiency. Built around exam-prep formula reference and worked problems.
  • Financial Accounting & Reporting Prof. Aleksander Aleszczyk · Mon/Wed 6–9pm · Jul 1 – Aug 5, 2026 (Midterm I Jul 13, Midterm II Jul 27, take-home final) The language of business, from the external user's seat: the four financial statements and how they articulate, debits/credits and T-accounts, accrual accounting and revenue recognition, adjusting entries and earnings quality — then receivables, inventory, PP&E, cash flows, liabilities, bonds, and equity. Built around journal-entry practice problems with worked solutions.

spring 2026

  • Strategic Communication Prof. Jason Turetsky · Section 32 · Jan 24 – Feb 1, 2026 (5-session intensive) Audience-first communication strategy: t+AIM, Plain English, presentation structure, persuasion, team dynamics. Built around a team case study.
  • Firms & Markets Prof. Nicholas Economides · Wednesdays 6–9pm · Feb 4 – Apr 29, 2026 (Exams Mar 25 & Apr 29) Microeconomics of firm strategy: markets, costs, monopoly, price discrimination, then game theory, price competition, cooperation, commitment, asymmetric information, platforms. Includes exam study hub and cheat sheet.
  • Leadership in Organizations Prof. Mike North · Section 30 · Mondays 6–9pm · Feb 2 – May 4, 2026 (LCP due May 10) Vision, leading change, generations, listening, courage, org design, persuasion, decision-making, networking, motivation, feedback, inclusivity. Built around cases (Mt. Everest, Naomi Chen, Wolfgang Keller, Lincoln Electric) and the capstone Leadership Commitments Plan.
